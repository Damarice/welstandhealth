Our Process

Our Rehabilitation Journey

We follow a structured and client-centered approach to ensure effective and personalized therapy outcomes.

01

Initial Assessment

We begin with a thorough assessment to understand your unique needs, goals, and current functional abilities.

02

Personalized Goal Setting

Based on the assessment, we collaborate with you to set clear, achievable, and meaningful therapy goals.

03

Individualized Treatment Plan

A customized treatment plan is developed, incorporating the most effective occupational and physio therapy techniques.

04

Therapy Implementation & Monitoring

We actively implement the plan, continuously monitoring your progress and making adjustments as needed.

05

Home Program & Education

We provide home exercise programs and education to empower you to continue your progress outside of sessions.

06

Outcome Evaluation & Discharge

Upon reaching your goals, we conduct a final evaluation and provide recommendations for maintaining your improved function.
