About Welstand

Dedicated to building independence and enhancing quality of life through personalized rehabilitation services

Our Story

Founded in 2019, Welstand emerged from a vision to transform rehabilitation services by focusing on independence and capacity building across the lifespan. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful belief: every individual deserves personalized, compassionate care that enhances their ability to live life to the fullest.

Built on the foundation of evidence-based practice and genuine care, we've grown from a small team of dedicated professionals to a comprehensive rehabilitation service provider. Our commitment to excellence and individualized care has made us a trusted partner for families and individuals seeking quality occupational therapy and physiotherapy services.

What sets us apart is our holistic approach to rehabilitation. We don't just treat conditions; we empower individuals to achieve their goals, build confidence, and reclaim their independence through well-thought-out and carefully planned programs.

Our Foundation

The principles that guide everything we do

Mission

To promote and maintain health, build capacity, prevent disability, increase independent functioning and enhance development through quality and individualized occupational therapy and physiotherapy services.

Vision

To be the leading provider of rehabilitation services, empowering individuals across their lifespan to achieve optimal independence and quality of life through innovative, compassionate care.

Purpose

Building independence and capacity for all across the lifespan through personalized, evidence-based therapeutic interventions that restore function and enhance participation in meaningful activities.

Our Core Values

The values that shape our approach to care and guide our daily practice

Compassion

We approach every client with empathy, understanding, and genuine care for their well-being and journey.

Integrity

We maintain the highest ethical standards in all our interactions and deliver services with honesty and transparency.

Collaboration

We work closely with clients, families, and healthcare teams to achieve the best possible outcomes through partnership.

Inclusivity

We ensure that all individuals, regardless of background or condition, receive equal access to quality care and respect.

Excellence

We continuously strive for the highest standards in service delivery and professional development.

Innovation

We embrace new approaches and technologies to enhance our therapeutic interventions and client outcomes.
