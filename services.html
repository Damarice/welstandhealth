Rehabilitative Services

At Welstand, we provide personalized and inclusive therapy services tailored to meet the unique needs of individuals across all age groups—enhancing independence, capacity, and quality of life.

Specialized Care
Assessment

Comprehensive Assessments

Identifying client strengths and needs to guide therapy planning.

  • Sensory Assessment – Understands how a person reacts to sensory input.
  • Functional Assessment – Evaluates ability to perform daily activities.
  • Cognitive Assessment – Assesses memory, attention, and problem-solving.
Therapy

Occupational Therapy

Helping individuals gain independence in everyday activities.

  • Early Assessments – Identifies areas needing intervention.
  • Intervention Planning – Creates personalized therapy goals.
  • Training & Support – Builds life skills for home and work.
Therapy

Physiotherapy

Improving movement, strength, and physical function.

  • Manual Therapy – Uses hands-on techniques for pain relief.
  • Exercise Therapy – Strengthens muscles and mobility.
  • Education & Advice – Guides long-term physical wellness.
Therapy

Speech & Language Therapy

Helping individuals overcome communication and swallowing challenges.

  • Speech Therapy – Improves articulation and fluency.
  • Language Therapy – Enhances vocabulary, grammar, and social communication.
  • Swallowing Therapy – Supports safe eating, drinking, and swallowing.
Specialized

Hand Therapy

Restoring hand function after injury or surgery.

  • Stretching & Mobilization – Improves flexibility and range.
  • Strengthening Exercises – Rebuilds hand control and grip.
  • Daily Task Training – Helps relearn functional use of hands.
Specialized

Sensory Integration

Helping individuals manage sensory input for better daily functioning.

  • Sensory Evaluation – Identifies sensory processing issues.
  • Therapeutic Activities – Supports regulation and coordination.
  • Family Education – Teaches strategies for support at home.
Specialized

Home Therapy & Programs

Delivering therapy services in the comfort of home.

  • In-Home Visits – Provide one-on-one therapy at home.
  • Custom Exercises – Tailored to the individual’s environment.
  • Family Support – Guides caregivers through therapy routines.
Specialized

Reintegration Support

Supporting return to school, work, and social life after recovery.

  • Transition Planning – Prepares individuals for next life steps.
  • Support Networks – Connects with peer or vocational groups.
  • Skill Readiness – Builds academic, work, and social skills.
Specialized

Home & Office Assessment

Making living and workspaces safe and accessible.

  • Barrier Evaluation – Identifies physical and ergonomic risks.
  • Workstation Adjustments – Improves comfort and posture.
  • Safety Training – Promotes injury prevention and good habits.
